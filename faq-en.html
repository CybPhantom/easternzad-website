What services does Eastern Zad provide?

Temperature-controlled, chilled, and frozen transport; storage and distribution; and logistics between warehouses, branches, and points of sale. The appropriate service is confirmed after reviewing the product, route, volume, and frequency.

What temperature range is available?

The published overall range is +18°C to -40°C depending on product and vehicle. A shipment uses the approved product setpoint, not the full range.

Is +18°C considered refrigeration?

It is temperature-controlled transport for suitable products, not low-temperature refrigeration. The displayed bands are controlled +18°C to +5°C, chilled +4°C to 0°C, frozen -18°C, and deep frozen -30°C to -40°C.

What information is required for a quote?
  • Company name and product type.
  • Required service and temperature.
  • Pickup and delivery cities.
  • Quantity and shipment frequency.
  • Packaging, loading, and handling requirements.
Do you cover every city in Saudi Arabia?

Eastern Zad lists coverage in major regions and cities. Route and schedule availability are confirmed after receiving the pickup, delivery, and shipment details.

Can you transport food and pharmaceuticals?

Services support food and temperature-sensitive products. Each shipment is subject to product, packaging, temperature, documentation, and applicable regulatory requirements.

Are tracking and temperature monitoring available?

Vehicle tracking and temperature monitoring are listed services. Report access and frequency are defined in the quotation and service agreement.

How do I start?

Send a complete quote request. The team reviews the product, route, capacity, and temperature before confirming details, pricing, and scheduling.

Who is responsible for packaging and loading?

Responsibilities are agreed before service. Packaging must suit the product, required temperature, and journey, with all necessary documents and handling instructions.

How can I submit a question or complaint?

Use the official contact channels on the website. Include the company name, order or shipment reference, and a clear description. Do not send passwords, financial details, or unnecessary personal documents.
